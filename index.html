🎯 Your Split: PPL A/B (6-Day Rotating)

Push A → Pull A → Legs A → Push B → Pull B → Legs B → Rest → Repeat. Every muscle hit twice per cycle with different exercises. No conventional deadlifts — RDLs do the job safely. Shoulders integrated into both push days.

💊 Creatine Protocol

Dose: 5g daily — consistency beats timing.

Loading? Optional. 20g/day × 5–7 days, then 5g. Or just start at 5g and saturate in ~4 weeks.

Hydration: +500ml water daily. Creatine pulls water into muscle cells.

📈 Progressive Overload Rules

Hit top of rep range on all sets → add weight next time.

Can't hit bottom of rep range → keep weight, fix form.

Add 2.5kg upper / 5kg lower body per progression.

If RPE 10 every set — you're overdoing it. Back off 5% and rebuild.
